Welcome to Home Page!

The Lost and Found Hub is a centralized online platform that offers a user-friendly experience for campus students to effortlessly report and recover lost items, saving students time and stress while ensuring a safe and dependable environment.

Problem to Solve

Every year, students lose valuable items like headphones, laptops, personal items in places such as the library, classrooms, food courts, etc. The process of recovering lost items is inefficient and time-consuming because most students are unaware of where to locate their items on campus. This is causes panic, stress, and wasted time for students who have no reliable source to look for their lost items on campus.

For instance, George Mason University has an information desk where students could potentially find their lost items; however, students have no visibility of items and have limited access due to specific operating hours. Other issues include distance which can be inconvenient for students with busy schedules. It is also inefficient in terms of communication because students would have to rely on reaching the staff at the information desk which can be difficult and busy at times. In addition, the information desk does not accept certain items such as glasses, clothing, vehicle keys, earbuds, etc. This is because the information desk has limited space; therefore. students would not be able to find such items that are still considered valuable to them.

Key Points

  1. Inefficient and time consuming for students
  2. Limited access due to time restrictions
  3. Unavaliability of lost items due to lack of space
