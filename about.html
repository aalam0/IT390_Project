About Lost and Found Hub

The Lost and Found Hub is a centralized online platform that offers a user-friendly experience for campus students to effortlessly report and recover lost items, saving students time and stress while ensuring a safe and dependable environment.

Proposed Solution

The Lost and Found Hub is a great place to allow students to come together and recover their lost possessions. The platform would allow students to either report and/or claim missing items. It provides organization and coordination, so that students can have immediate information whether their item has been found or not. There would no limitations in terms of availability of items, operating hours, etc.

Students would be able to easily search for items and communicate with other students on the online platform. Students would not have to revisit the information desk or wait for a call back from the staff for an update. The online platform would use automation to send notifications to the students once their items has been found. This IT solution would be more efficient than using traditional ways of claiming lost items across campus.

Key Points

  1. Immediate information provided available for students
  2. Organized and easily searchable
  3. Send notifications and/or alerts when lost items are found
